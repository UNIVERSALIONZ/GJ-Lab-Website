About Our Lab

The Bioprocess and Metabolic Engineering Lab (BME) at IIT Madras, led by Dr. Guhan Jayaraman, focuses on developing innovative bioprocess technologies and metabolic engineering strategies for sustainable production of biochemicals, and biofuels.

Our research integrates systems biology, synthetic biology, and bioprocess engineering to design and optimize microbial cell factories for industrial applications
